Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Operator: Data ISC · Belgium

1. Who we are

Data ISC ("we", "us") is operated from Belgium and subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation EU 2016/679) and Belgian implementing legislation.

2. What data we collect

Account data: email address and hashed password when you register.

Usage data: pages visited, scenarios selected, time on site — collected only if you accept analytics cookies.

Payment data: handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe); we never store card numbers.

Technical data: IP address (anonymised within 24 h), browser type, referring URL — processed for security and fraud prevention.

3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract (Art. 6.1.b): processing your email to provide the service.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f): security logs, fraud prevention.
  • Consent (Art. 6.1.a): analytics cookies — only after you click "Accept all".

4. Data retention

Account data is kept for the duration of your account plus 30 days after deletion. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised after 14 months. Payment records are retained 7 years as required by Belgian accounting law.

5. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing of your personal data. To exercise any right contact us at privacy@dataisc.dev. You may also lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (APD/GBA) at dataprotectionauthority.be.

6. Third-party processors

We use Stripe (payments, Ireland/US). Data transfers outside the EEA are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Changes

We will notify registered users of material changes by email at least 30 days in advance.
