Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Operator: Data ISC · Belgium

1. Who we are

Data ISC ("we", "us") is an open-source economic simulation tool operated from Belgium, subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation EU 2016/679) and Belgian implementing legislation.

2. No accounts, no registration

Data ISC requires no account and collects no registration data. There is no login, no email address required, and no password stored anywhere.

3. What data we collect

No tracking. Data ISC collects no analytics, usage, or behavioural data. There are no tracking cookies and no telemetry.

Technical data: if you self-host or access a hosted instance, the server may log your IP address (anonymised within 24 h) and browser type for security purposes only. The GitHub-hosted version (dataisc.github.io) is subject to GitHub's own privacy statement.

AI provider keys: if you use the AI assistant feature, your API key is entered and stored exclusively in your own browser (localStorage). It is sent directly from your browser to the AI provider — it never passes through our server.

4. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f): anonymised technical logs for security, if applicable to your deployment.

5. Data retention

Anonymised server logs, where applicable, are deleted after 90 days. localStorage values exist only in your browser and are never transmitted to us.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing of your personal data. To exercise any right contact us at privacy@dataisc.dev. You may also lodge a complaint with the Belgian Data Protection Authority (APD/GBA) at dataprotectionauthority.be.

7. Changes

Material changes to this policy will be announced via the project's GitHub repository.
