Commercial licence

A commercial licence removes the AGPLv3 copyleft requirement and grants you rights to deploy, integrate, and modify the software in proprietary products and services without open-sourcing your own code. Commercial licences are available for:

  • Standard: internal corporate tools, small-team SaaS deployments
