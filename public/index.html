📖 About the Model
How to use Data ISC

1 · Explore the demo

The tool opens with the built-in GDP Trajectories demo — 100+ countries projected from 1990 to 2100.

  • Switch between Map, Chart, and Race views using the buttons below the title
  • Drag the year slider to scrub through time on Map and Chart views
  • On Chart, use Top 10 or Trajectories to compare entities
  • Click any country on the map for a detail popup

2 · Bar Chart Race

Switch to the Race view to watch rankings animate from 1990 to 2100 in real time.

  • Hit ▶ Play to start — use speed controls to adjust pace
  • Active scenarios reshape trajectories live from their start year onward
  • Smart callouts surface leader changes and milestones automatically
  • Export as a WebM video in 16:9, 1:1, or 9:16 for social media

3 · Import your own data

Click Upload your dataset to load any time-series file.

  • Supported formats: CSV, XLSX, JSON
  • The tool auto-detects entity, time, and value columns — no fixed schema required
  • Works with any domain: economics, stocks, clinical data, environmental data, and more
  • After upload, confirm the column mapping and the tool switches to your data automatically

4 · Connect an AI provider

Click the AI button in the top-right to connect a provider. Free options are available.

  • Gemini Flash (Google) — free tier, no credit card
  • Groq — free tier with fast inference
  • Ollama — fully local, no internet required
  • OpenAI and Anthropic also supported (paid)
  • Your API key is stored in your browser only — never sent to our servers

5 · Run what-if scenarios

Describe a scenario in plain language — the AI estimates its impact and applies it to the projection.

  • For the GDP demo, pick a preset or type a custom scenario in the sidebar
  • For imported data, define a scenario in the Upload panel
  • The AI returns per-entity impact estimates with rationale — review and override before applying
  • Without AI, keyword analysis provides a basic estimate

6 · Export results

Use the Export ▾ menu to download your results.

  • PNG — snapshot of the current chart view
  • CSV / JSON — full projection data for the active scenario
  • Power BI ZIP — star schema export ready for DirectQuery
  • Video — export the Race as WebM in 16:9, 1:1, or 9:16
