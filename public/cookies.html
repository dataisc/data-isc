Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Applies to dataisc.dev and dataisc.github.io

No tracking cookies

Data ISC uses no analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies of any kind. There is no cookie consent banner because none is needed.

Browser storage we use

We use localStorage — stored locally in your browser only, never sent to a server — solely to remember your in-app preferences across page reloads:

KeyPurposeStored where
themeRemembers light / dark mode preferenceYour browser only
aiConfigStores your AI provider key and model selection (if you use the AI assistant)Your browser only
wes_view / wes_scenario / wes_active_dsRemembers your last active view, scenario, and datasetYour browser only
ws_display_ccyRemembers your selected display currencyYour browser only

None of these values are transmitted to us or any third party. You can clear them at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Legal basis

localStorage used solely for user-interface preferences is strictly necessary and does not require consent under the GDPR ePrivacy rules.
