Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Applies to dataisc.dev

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. We use browser localStorage (technically not HTTP cookies but treated identically under GDPR ePrivacy rules) for session management and preferences.

NameTypePurposeDuration
ws_sessionEssentialKeeps you signed inSession

Your choices

You can change your cookie preference at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer. Refusing analytics cookies has no effect on your ability to use the simulator.

Legal basis

Essential cookies: legitimate interest / strictly necessary (no consent required). Analytics cookies: consent (GDPR Art. 6.1.a / ePrivacy Directive).
